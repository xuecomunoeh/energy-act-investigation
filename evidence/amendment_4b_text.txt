Amendment 4B to the National Energy Independence Act

Section 1: Strategic Mineral Reserves
(a) The Secretary of Energy shall establish a strategic reserve of critical minerals essential for domestic energy production, with priority given to coal and coal-derived products.
(b) The reserve shall be maintained at a capacity sufficient to meet at least 180 days of national demand for coal-based energy generation.
(c) In determining which minerals to prioritize, the Secretary shall consider the need to secure domestic energy foundations and reduce reliance on foreign sources.
